All Natural.

IN OUR NATURE is a clothing and flower shop founded by Kerrel and Nkemdi Thompson. The in-house brand was birthed to bring forth reverence to the unspoiled natural beauty of vast outdoor landscapes, through more thoughtful practice of heritage craftsmanship and clothing design. IN OUR NATURE is a conduit for us to establish an outpost in our immediate community that exemplifies what we wish to see ripple across the Earth.

Established in 2018 but conceived long before, we've wanted to occupy a public space that serves as an identity for people, in places where they are less represented. In saying that we believe fashion has the means to affect our community first by making the inaccessible, accessible, using less (waste) and only sharing what we wholeheartedly believe in.

As a small family: just us and our 2 year old son Eames, we keep our studio and store close so we can have an direct impact on how our pieces are made while sharing the ability to reveal its value through the process. This is essential to why we do 100% of our dyeing in house, as well as other handcrafted features applied to our garments. Our ideas come as much from the possibilities we see from fabric and design techniques as our commitment to living engaged lifestyles and sharing Good News.
