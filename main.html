    MANIFESTO DYE STUDIO FLOWERS

IN OUR NATURE is a clothing and flower shop founded by Kerrel and Nkemdi Thompson. The in-house brand was birthed to bring forth reverence to the unspoiled natural beauty of vast outdoor landscapes, through more thoughtful practice of heritage craftsmanship and clothing design. IN OUR NATURE is a conduit for us to establish an outpost in our immediate community that exemplifies what we wish to see ripple across the earth.
